About Us

African Transformers Ghana Limited Company was founded in the year 2012. ATL is a Ghanaian registered company incorporated in Ghana and registered under company act 1962 (ACT179). It is wholly a Ghanaian company with Mr. Allaji Yusif Ibrahim as its Executive Chairman. Other Executive Directors are Mr. Jamil Ibrahim and Mr. Rasheed Ibrahim. The company is registered and authorized to manufacture transformers. The Company has its factory and it is located in Kpone Area, Tema. The factory Manager is Mr. Mayank Saini.

ATL have a 5000sq meter working area and total area 14000 sq mts in Tema industrial area. Capacity to produce 2000 transformers annually depending on the requirement For short range like 10kVA/50kVA single phase we have the capacity for around 5000 transformers annually average and for medium range like 100kva 200kva 315kva 500kva 2000 nos transfomers annual average. We have a 2500sq meter area for working 5ton crane and 2500sq meter area for working 10ton crane, along with 3 ton forklift, this specific quality of our company can increase our production capacity very high.
